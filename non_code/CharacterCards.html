Cardmaster

In exchange for the ability to fight on his own he enhanced his ability to support other people and things. Here are all the requirements and restrictions

  1. A card can never be used on the user himself except for teleporting ones.
  2. Once the card has been used it can’t be used again until the enemy is defeated or escapes
  3. there are 3 types of cards offensive, utility and support
  4. The user can wield weapons that have been applied with a card without performance issues (If the fire aspect is casted on a sword the user can fight with it but if he casted it on his arm it wouldn't work)
  5. a maximum of 2 cards can be used per turn
  6. when the gate card is casted first all the cards effects get enhanced
  7. Other people can use the cards if they get into possession of them ot use the whole deck if granted access by the user himself
  8. Abilities of other people can be turned into cards if they lose to phantom or one of the other users but first they have to agree for the consequences of losing.

Advanced battle form

In this form Leon gets two antennas on his forehead and an elytra like back armor under which he has an external sack of energy he harnesses from the environment. During this state he gets access to his most powerful technique “energy deficit”

Energy deficit

When it's activated Leo starts to absorb all energy in his surroundings. Whatever it's electricity or heat he stores it all. Additionally he can infuse his fists or a weapon with an aura that when in contact sucks even more kanyu. The downside of this technique is the extreme strain it puts on the body. 30 minutes in this state and the user will suffer full drawbacks of using max output, which means he will most likely never use kanyu ever again. Every punch sucks and expels sura at the same time which makes the users kanyu reserves fluctuate which leaves additional damage.

Energy annihilation

When user stores enough energy in the energy sack he can focus all of it and channel through a single body part (for example a finger) and unleash a powerful burst of energy that will annihilate any obstacle in its way. This attack will not only destroy a body part that is used but also force the body to use the max output. During a charge up kanyu gets sucked from other parts of the body quickly which can lead to necrosis.

Introduction (by the character itself):

Backstory: Kajdaiku was born in a family of foresters but when his mother died the father didn’t take it lightly. One day young Kajdaiku at the ripe age of 8 broke a wooden fork. He was determined to fix his mistake so the next week he was carving a new utensil but he wasn’t satisfied with it so he sneaked into a local blacksmith’s house and “borrowed some metal”. His father was frequently leaving him there when he needed a babysitter so a boy knew more or less what to do. When everybody learned about what the boy did they were astonished. He was better than every single apprentice, he was faster, smarter more efficient and creative. His father and a blacksmith became intrigued and started to teach him a little bit more and more until he was better than his master at 15 years old. Boy was spending every single second of his free time learning more about this sacred art. Soon he got a chance to prove himself. A traveling warrior needed a new sword because his old one was made by a fraud and broke mid battle. He was hesitant but entrusted the forging to young Kajdaiku. It was the first time in his life when he saw such exotic and intricate materials. He spent a long time on the design and process it was his finest work yet he poured his whole heart and soul into it using every trick in his book until something awakened in him, in that moment all the worries left his body and all he was able to think about was the process, his faith in his abilities made the warrior exceed his wildest expectations. The sword was near unbreakable and sharper than any sword the warrior wielded before. He promised to recommend him to everyone he encounters as long as the sword won’t fail him. As years went by more and more customers started to come to the young Noroi for the finest equipment in the country. He quickly gained more and more fame which he really liked. The king himself entrusted him a contract to forge him a royal sword and a suit of armor he would fight wars in. The prize was huge but the cost of failure was death. The king said “If I die in this suit, the creator shall be treated the same way. Being suited in armor and killed the same way I died”. Everybody was threatened to death. Such a risk at the line for one person such a stress, everybody was scared to even look at the proposal, but Kajdaiku just laughed and called it “an easy money”. And so he did forge as was told in a contract. For the first test king himself wanted to wear it and ordered a crossbow bolt to be shot precisely at his heart. Everybody was scared except Kajdaiku. When the bolt was fired it just glanced off the armor as if it was redirected by some mysterious force. From that point onwards Kajdaiku was titled as the “Legendary blacksmith”. He was taking care of his family until his father died when he was 21 from that point onwards he had to take care of his younger brother that himself started to make a name for himself as a world’s greatest architecht
